6.01 Final: Robot Waiter

A robot waiter that can fetch your order of drinks. The robot uses Bayesian probabilistic models to analyze thermal camera imaging data and determine the number of fingers raised. Then, it uses an internal state machine combined with linear-time-invariant models to complete autonomous wall-following driving to fetch your order.
